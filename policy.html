Privacy Policy

At Prepaid NG, we value and prioritize the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our services. By accessing or using the Prepaid NG platform, you agree to the terms and practices described in this policy.

Information Collection:

We collect information provided directly by you when you register, make purchases, or use our platform. This may include your name, contact details, payment information, and other relevant data necessary for transactional purposes.

Usage and Purpose:

The information we collect is primarily used to process transactions, provide customer support, and improve our services. We may also use your information to communicate with you about updates, promotions, or important service-related announcements.

Data Security:

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. We use encryption techniques, secure servers, and strict access controls to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of your data.

Data Sharing:

We may share your personal information with third-party service providers who assist us in delivering our services, such as payment processors and data storage providers. However, we ensure that these entities adhere to strict privacy standards and use your information solely for the purposes outlined in this policy.

Legal Compliance:

We may disclose your personal information if required by law, legal process, or governmental request. We may also disclose information to protect our rights, safety, or property, or that of our users or the public, as permitted by applicable laws.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies:

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and gather statistical information about our website usage. These technologies help us analyze trends, personalize content, and improve our services. You have the option to disable cookies in your browser settings, although this may affect certain functionalities of our platform.

External Links:

Our platform may contain links to external websites or services that are not operated or controlled by Prepaid NG. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Children's Privacy:

Prepaid NG does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently obtained personal information from a child, we will promptly delete it from our records.

Data Retention:

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, or as required by law. When your information is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

Updates to Privacy Policy:

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to periodically review this policy to stay informed about how we handle and protect your personal information.

Consent:

By using the Prepaid NG platform, you consent to the collection, use, and processing of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us through the provided channels. We are committed to addressing your concerns and ensuring the privacy and security of your personal information on the Prepaid NG platform.
